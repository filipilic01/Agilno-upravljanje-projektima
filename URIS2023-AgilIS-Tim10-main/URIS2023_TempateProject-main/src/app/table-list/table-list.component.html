FAQ section

FAQ section provides answers to commonly asked questions about agile project management, helping users navigate through key concepts and practices.

How do I create a new project in the software?

To create a new project, navigate to the dashboard and click on the "New Project" button. Then, follow the prompts to fill in project details.

How can I add team members to a project?

To add team members, go to the project settings and select the "Team Members" tab. From there, you can invite team members by entering their name.

Is there a mobile app available for the software?

No, there isn't a mobile app.

How can I generate reports for my project?

Currently, this feature is not available. However, it will be one of the future functionalities that we will enable for our users. Stay tuned!

Is there a free trial available for the software?

Yes, there is a free trial available for new users. You can sign up for it on our website.

How can I get technical support?

You can get technical support by contacting our support team via email.
